About This Tax Calculator

This comprehensive tax calculator helps you estimate your federal income tax for the 2024-2025 tax years. It includes all major deductions, credits, and considers different filing statuses.

Key Features:

  • 2024-2025 Tax Brackets: Updated with latest IRS rates and brackets
  • Multiple Filing Statuses: Single, Married Joint/Separate, Head of Household
  • Standard & Itemized Deductions: Compare both options
  • Major Tax Credits: Child Tax Credit, EITC, Education credits
  • State Tax Estimation: Includes major state tax calculations
  • Retirement Contributions: Accounts for 401k, IRA, HSA contributions

Important Tax Terms:

  • AGI (Adjusted Gross Income): Gross income minus specific adjustments
  • Taxable Income: AGI minus deductions (standard or itemized)
  • Marginal Tax Rate: Tax rate on your last dollar of income
  • Effective Tax Rate: Total tax divided by total income
  • Standard Deduction: Fixed amount based on filing status
  • Itemized Deductions: Specific expenses you can deduct

2025 Standard Deductions:

  • Single: $14,600
  • Married Filing Jointly: $29,200
  • Head of Household: $21,900
