About This Tool

This QR Code Generator creates customizable QR codes for various purposes. You can generate QR codes for websites, text, contact information (vCard), WiFi networks, emails, and more. All QR codes are generated locally in your browser for privacy.

How to Use

  1. Select the content type (URL, Text, WiFi, etc.)
  2. Enter your content in the appropriate fields
  3. Customize the QR code appearance using the options
  4. Click "Refresh" to update the preview
  5. Download as PNG or SVG for your needs

Common Use Cases

  • Business Cards: Create vCard QR codes with contact information
  • Marketing Materials: Add QR codes to brochures and flyers
  • WiFi Sharing: Generate QR codes for easy WiFi access
  • Product Packaging: Link to manuals or additional information
  • Restaurant Menus: Create digital menu QR codes
