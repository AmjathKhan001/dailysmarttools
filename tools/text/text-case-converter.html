Text Case Converter

Convert text between different case styles: uppercase, lowercase, title case, sentence case and more.

How to use this tool

1. Enter or paste your text in the input field.

2. Select the desired case style from the dropdown.

3. Click the "Convert Text" button to transform your text.

4. Use the "Copy Result" button to copy the converted text to your clipboard.
